CAMSPARENT

Camsparent is an Android-App that simplifies taking one picture twice from the same viewpoint. By overlaying the first image - for example a shot by daytime - over the camera view, you can easily take the same photo - for example at nighttime - from the exact same view. Now you have the chance to crop them togerther and create a new magic image containing both photos.
